Degree Description

This degree aims to train professionals capable of designing, developing, and maintaining computer-based systems. It combines mathematical foundations, programming skills, computer engineering principles, and transversal competencies.

  • Computer Engineering: Fundamentals of Computer Engineering, Electronics and Computer Technology
  • Humanities: Logic and Critical Thinking, History of Science and Technology
  • Business & Skills: Business and Its Processes, Knowledge Management and Personal Skills
